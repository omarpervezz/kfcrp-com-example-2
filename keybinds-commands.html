Keybinds & Commands

This guide was written for the KFCRP Roleplay FiveM server. To sign up for KFCRP Roleplay, click here.

How to create a custom keybind:

  1. Press F8
  2. Type: bind KEYBOARD F3 "e dance"

How to remove a custom keybind:

  1. Press F8
  2. Type: unbind keyboard f3

Main Interaction Keys

  • Z : Quick Menu
  • ALT : Third Eye: Hover over items then left click to interact.

Key binds

  • 1-5: Hotkey Assignments (Press F2 and drag items to set)
  • Tab: Inventory
  • B: Point / Speed Limiter
  • X: Hands Up
  • Z: Quick Action Menu (Stop button inside)
  • H: Hotwire a car while inside
  • 7: Set GPS to active alert (Police, EMS, etc.)
  • ALT-7: Remove active waypoint from map.
  • ALT-8: Send GPS ping for backup request to those on duty. (Police & EMS)
  • U: Lock / unlock vehicle (Inside or next to vehicle; must be owner)
  • K: Open clothing radial menu and use seatbelt
  • J: Browse Trunk when outside of vehicle or Glovebox when inside vehicle
  • LEFT CONTROL-C: Crouch
  • SHIFT-C: Crawl
  • Z: Stop Animation / Abort
  • F1: Phone
  • F2: Inventory
  • F3: Animations (Z to stop)
  • F5: List Owned Vehicles
  • F6: Job Menu (Visit ‘Job Center’ to obtain a job first)
  • F7: Invoices
  • F10: Scoreboard
  • N: Push to Talk
  • ~: Toggle Player IDs
  • G: Police Radar and Plate Reader and Seatbelt
  • LEFT CTRL + ~: Toggle Minimap on Foot
  • LEFT ALT + ~: Toggle Voice Tags
  • Hold LEFT CTRL: Vehicle HUD
  • LEFT CTRL + LEFT ARROW: Open/Close Driver or Left Passenger Door
  • LEFT CTRL + RIGHT ARROW: Open/Close Right Passenger Door
  • LEFT CTRL + UP ARROW: Roll Up Window
  • LEFT CTRL + DOWN ARROW: Roll Down Window
  • LEFT CTRL + BACKSPACE: Seat Shuffle
  • LEFT CTRL + H: Open/Close Hood (inside vehicle)
  • LEFT CTRL + E: Toggle Engine or Sit
  • LEFT ARROW: Toggle Left Indicator
  • RIGHT ARROW: Toggle Right Indicator
  • Engine Always On: Hold F when exiting vehicle
  • Leave Door Open: Hold Shift+F when exiting vehicle
  • LEFT SHIFT + E: Tackle nearby player

POLICE & EMS: Vehicle Sirens/Lights

  • Toggle emergency lights: CAPSLOCK
  • Airhorn: E
  • Toggle siren: ALT
  • Browse sirens: R
  • Take-down Light: Shift-H
  • Control Panel: O
  • Lock Lights/Sirens: I
  • Helicopter Sirens: Q, R, and both at the same time

POLICE DRONE

  • /drone : Spawn the drone on the floor in front of you. Run it again to remove it.
  • Forward Arrow - Move the drone forward
  • Backward Arrow - Move the drown backward
  • Left Arrow - Tilt the drone left
  • Right Arrow - Tilt the drone right
  • Enter - Toggle live camera stream
  • Home - Toggle night vision
  • Delete - Toggle thermal imaging
  • Page Up - Move the drone up, increasing the altitude
  • Page Down - Move the drone down, decreasing the altitude
  • Insert - Automatically land the drone

  • /grappler remove

POLICE: In-car Radar

  • Press G to open the radar menu while in a police vehicle. Then, click “Toggle Radar” to enable the radar.
  • Button Meanings:
    • XMIT - Turns on/off the antenna.
    • OPP - Makes the antenna scan traffic in the opposite lane.
    • SAME - Makes the antenna scan traffic in the same lane of travel.
    • Set Fast Limit - Set the speed a car must be travelling to be detected.
    • Toggle Speed Type - Switch between MPH and KPH.
  • A beep will sound when a car is detected above the limit. Their top speed will stay in the box.
  • Remove previous vehicles saved speed and reset by pressing G.

POLICE: Night Vision and Thermal Vision

    Press E while in the Police helicopters Right-click to cycle between modes
