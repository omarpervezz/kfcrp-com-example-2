Server Rules

This guide was written for the KFCRP Roleplay FiveM server. To sign up for KFCRP Roleplay, click here

The Rules are straight forward

Any Ignorance of the rules will result in administrative action.

GENERAL

  1. We are a UK server. You must speak and understand English at all times.
  2. You must have a working microphone with no background noise and crystal clear output. Voice changers are acceptable, but they must be understandable and not overly loud, too quiet, or extremely high or low pitched.
  3. No RDM (Random Deathmatch)
    • You MUST threaten to kill for a REASON & VICTIM must act FIRST
    • You must initiate roleplay by threatening life before killing someone
    • No REVENGE RDM -- If you believe you were RDM'd, CALL AN ADMIN! Do /Report
    • Revenge RDM is just as bad as regular RDM and will be punished equally.
  4. Do not be toxic or breed toxicity of any kind.
  5. The use of glitches, exploits, or hacks is prohibited. If you find a glitch or exploit, please notify the admin team through a bug report on Discord.
  6. Abide by the Twitch Terms of Service (ToS), Twitch Community Guidelines, and Discord Terms of Service while in the city and Discord.

BAN PROCESS

Ban duration is at the discretion of the staff members, but generally speaking the process for a ban is as follows:

  1. First offence = 1 week ban
  2. Second offence = 1 month ban
  3. Third offence = Permanent ban
  • More severe offenses can result in a permanent ban. Examples of these may include hacking, exploiting, toxic behavior, abusive/malicious behavior, and mass VDM or RDM.
  • Upon receiving a ban, our discord bot will DM you with your ban reason and the length of the ban. You will need to have DMs enabled on Discord in order to receive this. Alternatively, you can inquire about your ban at ban.kfcrp.com. It is your responsibility to open a ban appeal at ban.kfcrp.com at the end of your ban if it is not automatically removed by our Discord bot.

RESPECT AND VALUE OF LIFE

  1. No KOS (kill on sight) unless it is an agreed-upon between gangs in your respected territory (see Gang Guidelines below) or both parties have made a mutual introduction and understand why combat is occurring. Do not RDM (random death match) or VDM (vehicle deathmatch). You must have a logical reason for killing someone.
  2. You must value your character's life and the lives of other characters at all times. Therefore, your character's personality and mannerisms should portray fear when faced with a life threat. For example, if you have a gun pointed at the back of your head, and you don't have a weapon drawn?, put your hands up.
  3. Do not harass or disrespect other members or management. Instead, show respect to other players and keep the real-life drama out of roleplay. Do not use derogatory remarks regarding race, national origin, gender, religion, disabilities, or sexual preference. Do not base a character's roleplay around being heavily toxic, aggressive, or discriminatory.
  4. You have the right to bear arms inside your home, business, or place of employment. Therefore, you may use deadly force inside your home, business, or place of work if your character's life is at risk. Home and business owners may choose to disallow this at their discretion. Police may ignore hostage-takers demands while at any police precinct.
  5. Do not rob, kill or take hostage any NHS members.
  6. Hits and bounties on other players are not allowed.
  7. No Shooting Surrendering Players: Any player who shoots or kills another player when they are clearly surrendering, either by having their hands up or through clear in-game communication, will be subject to COMS or in severe cases a BAN from the server. This rule is in place to ensure a fair and enjoyable roleplay experience for all members of the community. Violating this rule demonstrates a lack of respect for fellow players and disrupts the immersive environment that we strive to maintain. Repeat offenders will face longer bans or permanent removal from the server at the discretion of server staff.

OUT-OF-CHARACTER AND SAFE ZONES

  1. Do not have out-of-character discussions in-game unless located next to an admin.
  2. All locations associated with city events are considered safe zones during the entirety of the event. Hospital & any other GREEN ZONE (subject to change).
  3. You may not wield weapons, and you may not commit a crime of any kind inside a safe zone.

ROLEPLAY GUIDELINES

  1. Do not attempt to change how someone roleplays their character by forcing your roleplay styles on them. Likewise, please do not attempt to silence someone or talk over them while they are roleplaying. Roleplay is a two-way street, and we are here to create stories with each other, not listen to ourselves speak.
  2. Always engage in the roleplay that is taking place around you. Players who ignore other players should understand what goes around comes around.
  3. Do not kidnap, rob, or assault NHS or medical staff for any reason.
  4. Do not kidnap, rob, or assault reporters while reporting on the scene of a crime/event. Reporters must have video or audio equipment in hand and visible to be eligible for this exemption. (DO NOT ABUSE)
  5. Do not kidnap, rob, or assault mechanics who are out on a tow. Mechanics can defend their shops against threats and respond with aggressive force if held at gunpoint at their place of employment.
  6. TRY not to kidnap, rob, or assault new players.
  7. Hostages cannot be friends, associates or imaginary. NPCs (locals) can only be used as a hostage when there are 15 or less players in the city.
  8. Do not use plain speech when downed unless NHS inquires about your injuries. Instead, act as if your character has heavy wounds through your character's personality and mannerisms.
  9. If your character revives by any method other than a medical staff member or admin, you may not remember who or what killed you or any events that led up to your character's death.
  10. Do not return to the place of your death for at least 10 minutes.
  11. Do not seek revenge unless it is shared with you by another character through roleplay.
  12. Medical staff, police and reporters are not allowed to participate in criminal activity while on or off duty.
  13. If you trigger an NHS alert, it is required to wait until NHS arrives on the scene if they have confirmed they are en route.
  14. If you are an NHS, do not revive downed players on-scene (IF THE GUNFIGHT IS ACTIVE). Instead, it would be best if you transported downed players to a hospital for treatment.
  15. Do not impersonate law enforcement officers or medical staff. It is strictly prohibited. Please do not wear any clothing items that belong to police or medical staff unless your job permits it.
  16. Do not perform r4pe, erotic, mutilation, or any other extreme roleplay variation.
  17. Players can be identified by their face, outfit, or individual clothing items up to 24 hours after a crime or other event. Stripping off clothing items after a crime or other interaction can still warrant a detainment by Police for questioning if they have probably cause or reasonable suspicion that you were involved in a crime or other event. We recommend changing your entire outfit before and after a crime and refraining from wearing gang colors during a crime as that would implicate the entire group.
  18. Do not create fictitious business story-lines at the scene of a crime to mask very obvious criminal activities that are being performed. Everyone knows what you are doing, and failure to act your part as a criminal will be considered FailRP or Powergaming. Examples of this includes but is not limited to, AC Repair Personnel, Bank Vault Maintenance Techs, etc. The government and the justice system will never write official business plan documents for ventures like this.
  19. kfcrp staff has the right to evict the owner of any walk-in business if the business is not actively utilized for RP purposes and/or used to host city published events. Owning a walk-in business with an interior simply to use it for a stash is withholding RP from the rest of the city.

New Life Rule (NLR) [THIS RULE IS FOR 10MINUTES IRL TIME]

  • You forget everything.
  • You cannot return to the place you died at (FOR 10 MINUTES).
  • You cannot revenge kill anyone from a past life situation.
  • You cannot wait around the vicinity of where you died. You must go somewhere else.

DO NOT MENTION RULES DURING AN ACTIVE RP SITUATION IF YOU FEEL LIKE A RULE WAS BROKEN USE /REPORT DON'T BREAK CHARACTER

GANG GUIDELINES

To refer to Gangs Guidelines and Rules click here

POWERGAMING

  1. Do not force another player to kill off their character permanently.
  2. Do not force another player to empty their bank account, stashes, trunks, or any other personal storage container.
  3. Do not chain rob a player if you are part of a group of players. Only one player of the group may loot the victim.
  4. Do not use third-party programs to gain an advantage over other players.
  5. Do not have more than five (10) players in a standard gang. Group size limits ensure interactions remain fair and balanced. Police may only have up to 14 units in a roaming group or on scene, including Air 1.
  6. Do not stake out safe zones and rob players as they exit. (SAFEZONE CAMPING)
  7. Do not run into safe zones to avoid interaction with other players.
  8. Do not force other players to revive at a doctor for any reason.
  9. Do not be corrupt if you are an officer of the law.
  10. Do not combat log by logging out of the server in any way to avoid jail or a roleplay situation. However, if it is an emergency or your game crashes, let the other players know in Discord and log back in as soon as possible to finish the roleplay scenario.

METAGAMING

  1. Do not use any out-of-character information to benefit your character.
  2. The use of third-party communication programs is NOT allowed. That said, please try to utilize in-game communication methods such as radios, phone calls, and text as much as possible. Communicating in-game helps develop backstories and provides more immersion to everyone else.
  3. Do not stream snipe. You may not be in another player's live stream while logged in to the server.

CHARACTER CUSTOMIZATION

  1. Do not make parts of your body invisible or intentionally glitched
  2. Character names add a level of immersion and must avoid being disruptive. Examples of common inappropriate or disruptive RP names include but are not limited to: a. Names that reference known people, characters, brands, places, or icons b. Names that reference slang terms, offensive language, or are just unoriginal. c. Names that consist of multiple words
  3. Do not stream snipe. You may not be in another player's live stream while logged in to the server.

ASSOCIATIONS

  1. Association with previously banned players can result in a ban and deletion of your account data depending on the circumstances and toxicity of the association.

DISCIPLINARY ACTION

  1. Do not make parts of your body invisible or intentionally glitched
  2. You can appeal bans at ban.kfcrp.com. If you are banned from the appeals Discord, the admin team has deemed your ban permanent, and you may not join the city again.

ABUSE REPORTS

To report another player, please use the #create-ticket channel on Discord. Press the tilde (~) key in-game to determine a player's server ID.

BUGS

In-game currency and item refunds are not distributed for bugs. However, our admin team will sometimes reward players who report bugs. Please use the ticket system in Discord to create a bug report.

DISCORD

  1. Discord is considered OOC (Out of Character). Therefore, topics discussed there should never be used in-game.
  2. Do not send friend invites or DMs to other players who have not solicited them.

OTHER RULES

  1. ANTICHEAT: To get UNBANNED after being banned by the Anticheat, you MUST have a clip. Also purposely shooting gas tanks to get banned will result in your remaining banned.
  2. No trading or selling Donator Cars, Tebex purchased items or Gang Whitelist.
  3. Once initiation starts, it's active for 2 minutes, afterwards you must reinitiate.
  4. No HOT REPAIRING (E.G: Repairing in the middle of a police chase WHILST MOVING).
  5. When a gang member is being arrested, ONLY THE SAME GANG WEARING THE SAME GANG ATTIRE can intervene.
  6. You CANNOT break out gang members other than your own from the POLICE/PD.
  7. PD can arrest/detain you if you are freely running around with your weapon of any class visible.
  8. Hitting someone with a snowball is classed as initiation.
  9. Do NOT abuse the /th/carry/escort command to fit extra people in your vehicle.
  10. Only ONE person can be in the trunk of a car.
  11. You CANNOT force a hostage to withdraw money from their bank.

INITIATION RULES

  1. Aiming at someone is INITIATION. (This includes police they can shoot if they feel a threat to life in any way.)
  2. Verbally saying "(gang name) K" creates active initiation with that gang -- VICTIM MUST ACT FIRST --
  3. No breaking FearRP. You must comply and fear for your life if you are alone against 3 or more people (IT MUST BE 3 GUNS TO THEIR HEAD AT ALL TIMES).
  4. You are NOT allowed to force someone to E-RP.
  5. If you don't have a weapon out and a gun pointing at your head, YOU MUST COMPLY.
  6. You can counterattack any group IF you have 2 OR MORE people.
  7. A TASER is NOT equal to a weapon. YOU MUST COMPLY if you have a taser out vs a gun.
  8. EX: - 1v2 = Can attack - 1v3 = MUST FEAR - 2vsANY = Can attack
  9. Shooting tires is INITIATION (Victim MUST act first).
  10. Lockpicking someone's car is INITIATION (Victim MUST act first).
  11. Stealing someone's vehicle is INITIATION (Victim MUST act first).
  12. Tazing is INITIATION (Victim MUST act first).
  13. 2 Slaps = INITIATION.
  14. Hitting someone with your vehicle 2 times or more is INITIATION.
  15. You CANNOT announce that you're with someone AFTER initial initiation.

GANG INITIATION RULES

  1. Gang initiation only applies within the area of initiation.
  2. If your gang member is in an active scene with the police, you MUST RP to get them out. Meaning you CANNOT just open fire.
  3. If a cop does use Semi-lethal to Lethal Force you can then open fire and begin a shootout with the police.
  4. (The area of initiation is ONE BLOCK WITHIN THE SURROUNDING BLOCK).
  5. Gang initiation lasts 2 MINUTES.
  6. Allied gangs do not apply with these initiations unless they are present and ANNOUNCE that they are with your initiation.

NOTE: YOUR GANG MUST BE IN ATLEAST TWO PIECES OF GANG CLOTHING FOR YOU TO BE COUNTED AS A "GANG MEMBER" - Shoes and Watches are NOT gang clothing and if only one person is wearing it then its not classed as your uniform.

ROBBERIES

  1. Only 3 outside shooters for BANK ROBBERIES.
  2. Only 2 outside shooters for JEWELRY HEISTS.
  3. You CANNOT have outside shooters for gas station/Liquor store/legion museum heists.
  4. Only ONE robbery may take place at a time.
    • Example: Fleeca bank robbery is active, you must wait until its done to start another heist.
  5. The maximum amount to rob another person for CASH is £100,000.
  6. You must wait at least 30 minutes before robbing the same person.
  7. You must announce you have an outside shooter (and how many) to police.
  8. You must fear your own life as a hostage.
  9. Must be 3 or more cops online to take one hostage.
  10. You may keep a hostage for a maximum of 15 minutes.
  11. You cannot leave a hostage alone whilst a bag is on their head.
  12. No fake hostages.
  13. You cannot kill a compliant hostage.
  14. You cannot scam more than £100,000. If it's more than this amount and you say "locked," you have to pay no matter the circumstances. (FAILING TO PAY WILL RESULT IN A BAN, WAGERS INCLUDED, YOU MUST HAVE A CLIP OF THE DEAL)
  15. You must wait 30 minutes before starting another heist.
  16. You are not allowed to respawn in active PD scenes. You must wait until you are searched or when all PD is dead and the scene is over. (They do not have to say "don't see the light")
  17. You must initiate with 3 guns pointing at the head before you can tie hands or put a bag over someone's head.
  18. You can rob someone for their inventory, but they must have their hands up and 3 guns pointing at the head. If you're not wearing 2 pieces of the same gang clothing, you must announce you are together BEFORE you initiate.
  19. Only money can be robbed from someone during /TH or tied hands situations.

DRUG LOCATIONS | TAXING

  1. Gangs/Crews can only tax at ONE drug location at a time.
  2. Must be at least 2 or more members present of a gang/crew to tax at a drug location.
  3. The maximum you are allowed to tax at a drug location is: 10 POUCHES.
  4. Do not kill someone for not paying taxes. You must give them a chance to leave first.
  5. You MUST reinitiate after entering/leaving a drug location.
  6. Do not run into a drug location to get out of an RP situation.
  7. You CANNOT 100k or die inside a drug location. You must tax them and give them time to either pay or leave.
  8. You CANNOT take a hostage inside drug locations REGARDLESS if their hands are up.

AGE REQUIREMENT

You must be 16 or over to play on KFCRP, If you are below this age you must get permissions from your parents and/or legal guardian.
